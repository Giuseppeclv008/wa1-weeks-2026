Why does my heap allocation fail when the input grows, even though the program works with small test cases?

by Me March 24, 2026

I'm testing a small C project and the application starts crashing with larger inputs. I suspect an allocation or boundary problem, but I would like advice on how to inspect and redesign the code more safely.

2026-06-01 Check the returned pointer from `malloc`, then verify every loop bound. If the bug only appears with bigger input, an out-of-bounds write is very likely. Me 5
2026-06-02 Use Valgrind or AddressSanitizer. They usually pinpoint the exact instruction that writes past the allocated memory block. Alex 8
2026-06-03 Consider replacing manual buffer management with safer abstractions, or at least centralize allocation logic in helper functions. Priya 6
